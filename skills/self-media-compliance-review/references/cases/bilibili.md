## Scenario Playbooks

Use these playbooks when the user asks `B站小黑屋怎么办`, `视频被锁怎么申诉`, `剪辑视频被锁`, `换源保留播放量`, or `新号被封原因`.

### 情况: 剪辑影视/动漫/游戏视频被锁、下架或申诉失败

Likely causes to check:

- Unauthorized use of film/TV/anime/game footage, music, voice, subtitles, screenshots, or creator material.
- Low-effort secondary creation: cropping, speed change, mirror, subtitles, simple stitching, no meaningful commentary.
- Cover/title highlights sensitive or copyrighted material more than the commentary.
- The original file/project was deleted, making appeal evidence weak.

First checks:

- Collect video id, lock/takedown notice, title, cover, tags, source list, original files, edit project, commentary script, authorization, and publish timeline.
- Check whether the video adds clear critique, explanation, education, or transformation.
- Inspect whether the cover/title uses official poster, actor portrait, dramatic scene, or misleading title.

What to do:

- Replace unauthorized material or reduce it to necessary quoted portions with meaningful commentary where allowed.
- Keep original project/source files and authorization before appeal.
- Rewrite cover/title to reflect commentary rather than pure clip viewing.

Appeal approach:

- Attach authorization or evidence of original commentary/transformation.
- Explain the value added and exact source segments used.
- Do not appeal only with "我剪了很久" or "别人也发了".

### 情况: 想换源、保留原地址/弹幕/播放量

Likely causes to check:

- The user is trying to preserve distribution after a violation, which can look like evasion if the root cause is not fixed.
- The replacement may still contain the same copyrighted or risky content.

What to do:

- Do not advise technical workaround first.
- Identify the violation category and fix the underlying issue: rights, sensitive frame, title/cover, source, or misleading content.
- Preserve original evidence and accept that re-upload or source replacement may affect data.

Appeal approach:

- If platform provides a formal replacement or appeal path, use it with corrected source and proof.
- Do not recommend black-screen replacement just to keep address, danmaku, or views.

### 情况: 新号或账号被封，原因不明

Likely causes to check:

- Account profile impersonation, abnormal registration/login, spam behavior, repeated comments, or associated-account history.
- Content posted immediately after registration triggers copyright, lowbrow, gambling/fraud, or diversion risk.

First checks:

- Account creation time, real-name/certification status, device/IP history, profile, first posts/comments/danmaku, private messages, and关联账号.
- Notice category and whether any content was removed before appeal.

What to do:

- Stop posting/commenting until cause is mapped.
- Clean profile/contact/diversion and preserve account-security evidence.
- If account was used only to repost existing content, prepare rights/source proof or remove the material.

Appeal approach:

- Submit account identity/security proof, content source proof, and explanation of first-use behavior.
- Avoid repeated generic appeals; each appeal should answer the specific notice.

### 情况: 标题封面党、热点误导、引战或人身攻击被处理

Likely causes to check:

- Title, cover, tags, or opening exaggerates drama, death/injury, crime, gender/region/group opposition, celebrity rumor, or public-affairs claim.
- The body later adds context, but the cover/title itself highlights violating or misleading content.
- Comments/danmaku become insults, doxxing, harassment, or organized attacks because the video frames conflict sensationally.

First checks:

- Compare title/cover/opening with the actual evidence in the video.
- Collect source links, screenshots, timeline, and comment/danmaku examples if the issue involves public affairs or disputes.

What to do:

- Rewrite title/cover to match the factual content and remove insulting, discriminatory, or rumor-like wording.
- Add source and context for public-affairs or controversy material.
- Moderate comments and danmaku that expose privacy or attack individuals/groups.

Appeal approach:

- Attach corrected title/cover, source proof, and comment cleanup evidence.
- Do not defend a misleading title by saying the full video later explains it.

### 情况: AI配音、AI换脸、虚构事件或合成素材被误认为真实

Likely causes to check:

- AI voice/face/image/video is used for celebrity, public event, accident, disaster, medical, finance, legal, or product-proof content without clear labeling.
- Synthetic screenshots or fake chat records are used as proof.
- Viewers in comments treat the content as real and the creator does not clarify.

First checks:

- Identify AI-generated frames/audio, source files, prompt/output records, and whether public labels are visible in title/description/subtitles.
- Review comments for confusion that suggests misleading presentation.

What to do:

- Add clear AI/synthetic labels in title, description, subtitles, or pinned comment where needed.
- Remove realistic fake evidence, public-affairs misinformation, celebrity impersonation, or product-proof deception.

Appeal approach:

- Submit generation records, corrected labels, and before/after screenshots.
- If the material is real, attach original shooting/source evidence and project files.

### 情况: 评论、弹幕、动态或私信引流资源/群聊

Likely causes to check:

- Comments, danmaku, dynamic posts, profile, or private messages direct users to网盘,群,QQ/微信,资源站,成人资源, game外挂, paid scripts, or off-platform purchase.
- The main video appears informational, but pinned comments or replies create a resource funnel.
- Repeated replies or danmaku look automated or spammy.

First checks:

- Review description, pinned comments, replies, dynamic posts, profile signature, private-message CTA, and any linked pages.
- Verify rights and legality of the resource if the creator claims it is legitimate.

What to do:

- Delete resource-channel, contact, and piracy/adult/外挂 directions.
- Replace with official purchase/download/source path if legitimate.
- Stop repeated copy-paste replies and document moderation cleanup.

Appeal approach:

- Attach screenshots of comment/danmaku cleanup and official source or rights proof.
- Do not use homophones, screenshots, or coded phrases to preserve the funnel.

### 情况: 直播、游戏外挂、抽奖赌博或交易内容被限制

Likely causes to check:

- Video or live content teaches cheating tools,外挂,代练,账号交易,虚拟币/装备私下交易, gambling-like抽奖, or paid ranking manipulation.
- Live cover/title/background exposes contact or off-platform payment.
- Comments solicit paid service, ban/unban,刷量, or private transactions.

First checks:

- Collect live replay, cover/title, game footage, tool links, product/service copy, comments, and transaction path.
- Identify whether the content is critique/education or practical instruction/sale.

What to do:

- Remove tool links, transaction instructions, private contacts, and gambling-like mechanics.
- Keep commentary factual and avoid teachable operational steps for cheating or evasion.

Appeal approach:

- Submit corrected live/video segment and comment cleanup evidence.
- Do not frame paid evasion or作弊 services as "技术教程".
