## Scenario Playbooks

Use these playbooks when the user asks `公众号封号怎么办`, `存在安全风险是什么`, `怎么申诉`, `恶意举报怎么办`, or `注册第一天被封怎么办`.

### 情况: 提示存在安全风险、账号被限制/冻结/封禁

Likely causes to check:

- Account security risk: stolen account, abnormal login, operator change, password leak, third-party tool, automation, or batch operation.
- Registration/certification risk: real-name missing, subject information inconsistent, qualification missing, account name/profile looks official or misleading.
- Content/business risk: illegal service, fraud-like copy, suspicious customer-service messages, external-link or mini-program destination risk.
- Associated-subject risk: same subject or related accounts have repeated violations.
- User complaint/regulator notice triggered platform handling.

First checks:

- Collect exact notice text, account id, subject/certification status, handling time, restricted function, appeal result, and whether followers/articles/functions were affected.
- Build a security timeline: login devices, IP/location alerts, password resets, operator changes, third-party authorization, plugin/tools, message-send logs.
- Check recent articles, menu links, template messages, auto-replies, customer-service messages, comments, external links, mini-program cards, and QR/contact variants.
- Verify qualifications for regulated services: medical, finance, legal, education, recruitment, fundraising, paid knowledge, or local services.

What to do:

- Secure the account first: reset password, remove unknown operators/tools, revoke third-party access, preserve login/device evidence.
- Remove risky links, auto-replies, menus, templates, comments, and articles before appealing.
- If real-name/certification/qualification is missing, complete or prepare proof before arguing content.

Appeal approach:

- Structure appeal as: "账号主体 + 风险发生时间线 + 已整改动作 + 证明材料 + 请求复核的具体功能/文章".
- Attach notice screenshots, subject proof, certification/qualification, login-device evidence, operator cleanup screenshots, article/link screenshots, and correction evidence.
- For stolen-account claims, emphasize abnormal login/device/IP and unauthorized content timeline; do not only say "不是我发的".

Do not do:

- Do not copy generic apology templates or claim a success rate.
- Do not buy `代申诉/解封` services.
- Do not register related subjects/accounts to continue the same business before identifying the cause.

### 情况: 文章删除、屏蔽、禁转发、原创声明被取消

Likely causes to check:

- Infringement or originality abuse: copied article, image, video, public information compilation, non-exclusive authorization, watermarked external-platform video, marketing article marked original.
- Content category risk: rumors, public affairs without source, AI content without label, vulgar/violent/gambling/fraud, privacy exposure, group opposition, over-marketing.
- Clickbait/diversion: incomplete title/cover, superstitious/lowbrow bait, unrelated mini-program card, external-link ad revenue funnel.

First checks:

- Identify article id/link, title, cover, publish time, original declaration status, source links, repost permission, and edit history.
- Compare with original materials and rights proof.
- Check whether source labeling uses an official source rather than vague `网传/网友表示/互联网`.

What to do:

- Remove unauthorized material or original declaration if the work is compilation/marketing/non-exclusive.
- Add authoritative source labels for public affairs, policy, social incidents, and AI-generated/synthetic content where required.
- Remove unrelated mini-program cards, forced follow/share, external-link bait, and misleading covers.

Appeal approach:

- If rights issue: attach source files, license/authorization, and originality explanation.
- If source-label issue: attach authoritative source and corrected article screenshot.
- If platform misunderstanding: cite the exact article segment and explain why it is compliant after correction.

### 情况: 被投诉或疑似恶意举报

Likely causes to check:

- The complaint may still match a real issue: defamation, privacy, copyright, misleading commercial claim, unauthorized portrait/name, or external-link fraud.
- Multiple similar complaints can trigger stricter handling if the account has prior records.

What to do:

- Do not assume the report is malicious. First map the complaint to article/title/cover/comment/link evidence.
- Preserve original article, logs, rights proof, source proof, and communication records.
- Remove or mask disputed personal information while appeal is pending.

Appeal approach:

- Provide rights/license/source proof and explain why the complained content is lawful and platform-compliant.
- If the complaint is false, attach counter-evidence and keep the tone factual.
