## Scenario Playbooks

Use these playbooks when the user asks `抖音封号怎么申诉`, `直播禁播怎么办`, `被扣保证金/收益怎么办`, `被恶意举报怎么办`, or `能不能举报/封别人号`.

### 情况: 评论区或私信出现 `封号/禁播/互举/付费举报` 服务

Likely causes to check:

- User comments are soliciting malicious reports, paid account bans, live-room bans, or coordinated reporting.
- The creator's content may be teaching举报技巧, anti-ban methods, or selling platform-evasion services.
- Comments may create fraud/diversion risk even if the main video is educational.

What to do:

- Delete or hide paid-ban, paid-report, `互举直播间`, and off-platform contact comments.
- Do not reply with contact details, price, or "私信".
- If the video discusses reporting, keep it factual: official complaint path, no paid service, no targeting, no promised outcome.

Appeal/risk response:

- If the account is punished for comments or service solicitation, provide screenshots of comment cleanup, moderation settings, and statement that no paid report/unban service is offered.
- Keep official-rule framing: reporting violations is allowed; paid malicious reporting or ban services are not.

### 情况: 账号封禁、直播禁播、处罚原因不清

Likely causes to check:

- Content category: sexual/lowbrow, minor harm, dangerous behavior, fraud/illegal ads, gambling, illegal finance, infringement, AI/deepfake without label.
- Account behavior: automation, fake interaction, batch accounts, abnormal login, account rental/sale/lending.
- Live behavior: off-platform transaction, contact exposure, fake lottery, high-risk product/service, repeated warnings.

First checks:

- Collect exact platform notice, account id, video/live id, violation category, current restricted function, appeal deadline, and prior warning history.
- Review title, cover, first 3 seconds, subtitles, comments, live replay, product link, account profile, and private-message CTA.
- Check whether third-party operators or agencies used scripts/tools.

What to do:

- Fix content first: remove contact/diversion, risky product claims, lowbrow frames, dangerous instruction, unlicensed professional advice, and copied material.
- Secure account if abnormal login/tool usage is suspected.
- Preserve original videos and live replay before deletion if needed for appeal evidence.

Appeal approach:

- Use evidence-specific appeal: notice + disputed material + correction + source/qualification/security proof.
- For live bans, attach live replay segment, product qualification, transaction path, and comment moderation evidence.
- Do not promise that a method "一定解封"; platform discretion remains.

### 情况: 未成年人、擦边、低俗或敏感封面被处理

Likely causes to check:

- Cover/opening uses minors, revealing clothing, dating implication, body focus, bullying, self-harm, violence, or sensational family conflict.
- Comments sexualize minors or invite contact/meetups.

What to do:

- Remove the risky frame from cover/opening and cut or blur sensitive scenes.
- Moderate comments and remove tags that sexualize or sensationalize minors.
- If the content is commentary/public-interest, keep source and safety context, not traffic bait.

Appeal approach:

- Attach corrected cover/title and explain public-interest or educational purpose.
- For serious minor sexualization/privacy/self-harm, treat as `Blocker` rather than appeal-first.

## Minor-Safety Governance

Media-reported platform signal: 2026-06 reports bundled Xiaohongshu and Douyin minor-protection announcements and referenced permanent bans for serious minor-related violations.

Review implication:

- Treat minor sexualization, dating inducement, violence, suicide/self-harm, bullying, privacy exposure, and `擦边` traffic around minors as `Blocker`.
- Do not use minor-related sensational frames as cover/opening even if the video claims to be commentary.
