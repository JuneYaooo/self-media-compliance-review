## Scenario Playbooks

Use these playbooks when the user asks `视频号限流怎么办`, `视频号封号怎么申诉`, `忘记标AI怎么办`, `疑似非原创怎么办`, or `直播/广告收益被处理怎么办`.

### 情况: 忘记选择 AI 创作、AI/合成内容被提示异常

Likely causes to check:

- AI face/voice/event footage may mislead users if not labeled.
- Synthetic public-affairs, disaster, accident, celebrity, medical, finance, legal, or product-proof content is high-risk.
- The creator may have removed or obscured platform labels.

First checks:

- Identify whether the video uses AI face, voice, generated scenes, synthetic product proof, or AI script/voiceover.
- Check title, cover, caption, subtitles, and comments for claims that imply the synthetic content is real.
- Preserve generation source, prompt/output metadata if available, and edit history.

What to do:

- Add clear AI/synthetic disclosure in title/caption/subtitle where appropriate.
- Remove realistic fake public-event, accident, celebrity, medical, finance, or legal proof unless unmistakably labeled and editorially necessary.
- Do not re-upload repeatedly until label and misleading claims are fixed.

Appeal approach:

- State that the content is synthetic, identify where labels were added, and attach screenshots of corrected public-facing labels.
- If the content was not AI, attach original shooting files, behind-the-scenes evidence, source file metadata, or project files.

### 情况: 疑似非原创、搬运、抄袭、大小号重复发布

Likely causes to check:

- Same/similar video was posted by another account, including the creator's own large account.
- Video contains watermarks, reposted clips, film/TV/game/anime material, or low-effort edits.
- Opening a smaller account after a larger account was restricted can look like evasion.

First checks:

- Compare source files, publish sequence, account ownership, authorization, and edit project.
- Check whether the video adds meaningful commentary or only cropping, speed change, subtitles, or simple edits.
- Collect proof that both accounts are operated by the same lawful主体 if relevant.

What to do:

- Remove unauthorized clips or add substantial original commentary/analysis if allowed.
- Do not repost the same punished video on a small account.
- Keep original files and project files before appeal.

Appeal approach:

- Attach source files, project timeline, account ownership proof, authorization, and explanation of original contribution.
- If duplicate across own accounts, explain account relationship and why it is not evasion; still fix any content risk first.

### 情况: 手机号/私域导流后限流或申诉失败

Likely causes to check:

- Video, caption, profile, comments, or private-message prompt exposes phone, WeChat, QR, homophone contact, or `评论/私信拿资料`.
- The content uses free资料/报价/课程/补课/咨询 as funnel bait.
- Regulated service, such as tutoring or paid consulting, lacks qualification or compliant transaction path.

What to do:

- Remove contact exposure and private-domain bait from video, caption, profile, comments, and pinned comments.
- Move legitimate transactions/consultations to platform-supported paths where available.
- Add qualification and service scope evidence for regulated services.

Appeal approach:

- Attach before/after screenshots showing contact removal, qualification proof, and corrected CTA.
- Do not appeal by saying "只是留个联系方式"; explain user-safety and transaction-path correction.

### 情况: 直播封禁、广告展示关闭、收益扣减、被判互动/广告数据异常

Likely causes to check:

- Uniform comments, comment brushing, fake interaction, batch likes, mutual engagement, or third-party traffic.
- `私信/评论拿资料`, welfare bait, fake lottery, or engagement manipulation.
- Live/short video links or comments create ad-data manipulation or misleading commercial funnel.

What to do:

- Stop engagement campaigns and remove scripted comments.
- Preserve live replay, comment logs, promotion records, ad-income notice, and team operation logs.
- Audit whether assistants, agencies, or fans were instructed to post uniform comments.

Appeal approach:

- Provide activity plan, organic traffic evidence, team instructions showing no brushing if true, and correction evidence.
- If brushing happened, state cleanup and prevention steps; do not blame unknown users without evidence.

### 情况: 播放量骤降、减少推荐、疑似限流但没有明确通知

Likely causes to check:

- The creator infers violation from low views only; there may be no platform notice.
- Cover/title/opening uses private-domain bait, exaggerated marketing, lowbrow conflict, copied素材, or repeated template.
- Account recently deleted/reposted frequently, changed niche, switched operators/devices, opened a small account after punishment, or reused the same video across accounts.

First checks:

- Capture video id, publish time, traffic graph, platform notice, search/recommend visibility, edit history, and recent account operation records.
- Review cover, first 3 seconds, caption, comments, product link, profile, and private-message prompts.

What to do:

- If no notice exists, mark `待核验: 推荐状态/账号状态` rather than calling it a confirmed violation.
- Fix visible risks first: contact/diversion, exaggerated claim, copied素材, repeated hashtags, and comment mobilization.
- Avoid mass deletion/reposting or switching to small accounts before the root cause is identified.

Appeal approach:

- Appeal only with a specific notice or function restriction; otherwise prepare evidence and observe after correction.
- If appealing, include video id, publish time, disputed frame/copy, correction screenshots, source proof, and account-operation timeline.

### 情况: 视频号小店、课程、咨询或本地服务被判虚假营销

Likely causes to check:

- 口播, caption, comments, live script, or product card promises guaranteed effects, income, traffic, scores, exam results, health outcomes, or "官方/内部" resources.
- Price, gift, service scope, refund terms, delivery deadline, or qualification differs across video, live room, comments, and product link.
- Education, tutoring, health, finance, legal, recruitment, or paid consulting lacks visible qualification or oversteps account identity.

First checks:

- Collect product card, order path, price/activity screenshots, qualification,服务合同, live replay, comment replies, and platform notice.
- Compare public claims with actual purchasable item or service.

What to do:

- Remove absolute promises and unverifiable numbers; rewrite as factual scope and conditions.
- Align product link,口播, comments, and客服话术.
- Add qualification proof or remove regulated advice/marketing.

Appeal approach:

- Attach corrected public copy, link consistency proof, qualification, and refund/service terms.
- Do not argue "只是经验分享" when comments or links create a sales funnel.

### 情况: 未成年人、擦边、家庭冲突或猎奇封面被处理

Likely causes to check:

- Cover/opening highlights minors, revealing clothing, dating implication, bullying, self-harm, violent family conflict, or sensational school/family scenes.
- Comments contain sexualized, bullying, doxxing, meetup, or "求后续" manipulation around minors or vulnerable people.
- The video claims public-interest commentary but uses the sensitive frame as traffic bait.

First checks:

- Review cover, first 5 seconds, subtitles, comments, tags, and whether identities/locations are exposed.
- Collect source authorization, guardian/portrait consent where relevant, and corrected cover/title.

What to do:

- Remove risky frames from cover/opening; blur faces, school, location, names, and comments.
- Delete harmful comments and avoid sensational titles.
- Keep educational or public-interest context visible if the topic is necessary.

Appeal approach:

- Appeal only after replacing cover/title and cleaning comments.
- For sexualized minor, self-harm, violence, or privacy exposure, treat as `Blocker` rather than appeal-first.
