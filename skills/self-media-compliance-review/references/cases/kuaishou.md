## Scenario Playbooks

Use these playbooks when the user asks `快手永久封禁怎么办`, `快手账号被盗申诉`, `直播一开播就违规`, `快手禁言怎么办`, or `回复资源被封怎么办`.

### 情况: 声称账号被盗后被永久封禁

Likely causes to check:

- Account actually had abnormal login/device/IP and unauthorized posting.
- Account owner used third-party tools, shared credentials, account rental/sale/lending, or weak password.
- Appeal failed because evidence only says "不是我发的" without login/device proof.

First checks:

- Collect ban notice, account id, violation category, offending content id/time, login device list, IP/location alert, password reset history, operator changes, third-party tool authorization, and customer-service replies.
- Build a minute-level timeline: normal last login, abnormal login, offending post, user discovery, password reset, appeal.

What to do:

- Reset password, remove unknown devices/operators/tools, enable security protection, and preserve screenshots.
- Do not delete all evidence before capturing offending content and notices.
- If illegal/porn/lowbrow content was posted by another party, identify exact content and explain why it conflicts with normal account history.

Appeal approach:

- Lead with security evidence, not emotional language.
- Attach abnormal login/device/IP screenshots, reset records, unauthorized content timeline, and cleanup actions.
- If the platform says it was not stolen, respond with concrete mismatch evidence: device model, location, time, operator, and normal usage pattern.

### 情况: 直播刚开播就出现违规记录，但客服说账号正常

Likely causes to check:

- Live cover, title, background, audio, clothing, product, pinned comment, or profile contains risky contact/diversion/lowbrow/commerce signals.
- Previous live or account behavior left risk markers even if current live is clean.
- Automation, replayed media, black screen, no真人互动, or looped product video can trigger live-commerce risk.

First checks:

- Capture live notice, live cover/title, first 60 seconds replay, product cards, profile, pinned comments, assistant comments, and moderation logs.
- Check whether the same cover/title/template is reused across multiple lives.

What to do:

- Replace cover/title, remove contact info, remove lowbrow or misery bait, ensure real-person interaction, and use platform transaction tools.
- Stop using copied appeal wording; ask support for the exact live scene/function affected if possible.

Appeal approach:

- Submit live id/time, screenshots of current clean cover/title/profile, replay segment, and changes made.
- Ask for review of a specific record rather than generic "恢复账号权限".

### 情况: 评论禁言或封禁，因为回复了资源渠道、联系方式、第三方平台

Likely causes to check:

- Comment mentions where to watch/read/download, third-party platforms, contact variants, pirated resources, adult resources, or paid services.
- Repeated copy-paste comments or resource sharing look like spam/diversion.

What to do:

- Delete or stop resource-channel replies.
- Replace with compliant answer: summarize publicly available information without directing to unauthorized resource locations.
- Review profile and pinned comments for the same contact/resource funnel.

Appeal approach:

- State the exact comment, why it was mistaken or how it was corrected, and promise no further resource diversion.
- If the content was legitimate, attach official source link or rights proof.

Do not do:

- Do not switch to homophones, screenshots, emojis, or code words for contact/resource channels.
- Do not buy unban services or submit mass copied scripts.
