## Severity Hints

- `Blocker`: law/policy red line, severe illegal/prohibited content, pornography, gambling, terrorism/extremism, gore/death, criminal method, controlled goods, minor sexual/violent harm, fraud, serious infringement, serious misinformation, unqualified high-risk industry, repeated malicious account behavior.
- `High`: likely deletion/downrank/restriction: lowbrow sexual implication, blood/horror, dangerous challenge, cyberbullying, privacy exposure, misleading title/cover, malicious marketing, AI/deepfake deception, harmful minors content.
- `Medium`: context-dependent; may be acceptable with education/science/news/art context, sufficient explanation, warning, masks, labels, source, and non-sensational handling.
- `Low`: title/cover/user-experience or wording issue.

## Platform Surface

Bilibili applies rules across:

- Videos and image/text posts.
- Cover, title, tags, descriptions, dynamic posts.
- Comments, danmaku, private messages, account nickname/avatar/signature.
- Live behavior and related links.

Cover and title are treated as the user's first impression; if they highlight violation content, Bilibili may handle more strictly. Do not use risky frames as cover/opening even when the full video has context.

## 1. Account and Behavior

Flag:

- Content or account behavior violates laws, public order/morals, community convention, others' rights, site operation, or platform order.
- Reposting the same content without fixing a previously punished issue.
- Serious, malicious, repeated, or disputed-account behavior.
- Fake data, coordinated manipulation, malicious reports, account trading, evasion through associated accounts.
- For fan accounts above large thresholds or "self-media" accounts, missing front-stage real-name display may create distribution/monetization restrictions when platform policy applies.

Potential handling includes deletion/offline, distribution limits, warning labels, folding comments, resetting profile info, live restrictions, account functional limits, freezing, or bans.

## 2. Illegal and Prohibited Content

Flag:

- Political/national-security red lines, sovereignty and map errors, damage to national honor/interests, state secrets.
- Ethnic/regional hatred, religious-policy violations, cults, extremism, terrorism.
- Historical nihilism, insulting heroes/martyrs, distorting party/state/military/police/public officials.
- Colonialism/fascism/racism glorification.
- Superstition for profit, supernatural claims that treat disease or change fate, fortune-telling/curses/ritual marketing.
- Controlled goods: guns, ammunition, drugs, chemicals, dangerous contraband, production/sale/use methods.
- Gambling, fraud, illegal finance, prohibited services, criminal method, or incitement.

## 3. Pornography, Vulgarity, and Lowbrow Content

Flag:

- Pornographic or explicit sexual content.
- Soft porn, sexual implication, sexualized framing, body-part focus, suggestive voice/text/actions.
- Vulgar language, obscene jokes, crude humiliation, lowbrow meme edits.
- Lowbrow content that erodes public values or promotes bad social atmosphere.
- Minors appearing in sexualized, suggestive, or inappropriate contexts.

For borderline anime/comic/game content, review cover, first seconds, camera focus, subtitle text, and audience age context.

## 4. Blood, Violence, Horror, and Discomfort

Flag:

- Severe injury, blood, exposed organs, mutilation, death, torture, abuse, fighting, animal cruelty.
- Horror/jump-scare material that causes strong discomfort.
- Sports/game/film clips that isolate violent or bloody moments for attention.
- Violent law-enforcement framing, one-sided misleading edits, or sensational casualty footage.

Educational, documentary, scientific, artistic, or explanatory use may reduce severity only when the purpose is clear, context is sufficient, and the sensitive visual is not used for stimulation.

## 5. Dangerous Behavior

Flag:

- Suffocation challenge, dangerous tools/weapons, high-risk stunts, self-harm, prank challenges, fire/electricity/chemical misuse.
- "作死", challenge, imitation, tutorial, ranking, or reward framing.
- Dangerous behavior used for marketing or traffic.

Fix by removing instruction/steps, adding non-sensational safety context, and avoiding dangerous footage as cover/title/opening.

## 6. Minors

Flag:

- Violence, sexual content, bullying, family violence, grooming, exploitation, or rights harm involving minors.
- Minors smoking, drinking, drugs, gambling, tattoos, cosmetic surgery, dropping out, entering adult venues, gang behavior, unsafe driving/challenges.
- Minor privacy exposure: name, face, address, school, route, family, ID, or health.
- Using minors for profit, commercial promotion, staged persona, long-term/high-frequency摆拍, controversy traffic, or behavior beyond age cognition.
- Content harmful to minors' values: cyberbullying, cheating, school bullying, extreme emotions, superstitious/anti-science claims, unhealthy diet.

## 7. Misinformation and Misleading Content

Flag:

- False or unverified public-event information.
- Missing source/time/location/person/causal chain for news-like content.
- Old events presented as new.
- Celebrity/public-figure rumor stitching.
- AI/deepfake accident/war/news footage without clear labeling.
- Logical fallacies or unsupported claims used to draw public-interest conclusions.
- Misleading title, cover, or text unrelated to the actual content.

## 8. Marketing and Commercial Promotion

Flag:

- Malicious marketing, spam, or promotion outside required process.
- False or misleading advertising, fake efficacy, fake authority, fake data, fake price/activity, fake scarcity.
- High-risk industries such as medical, finance, legal, education, health, games, fundraising, or other regulated domains without qualification.
- Content that exploits injuries, death, disease, poverty, minors, public incidents, or social conflict for traffic or product sales.
- Product link or CTA unrelated to the video.

## 9. Interaction and Public Order

Flag:

- Personal attacks, insults, profanity, threats, harassment, curses.
- Exposing personal privacy or encouraging human flesh search.
- Discriminatory attacks based on region, ethnicity, gender, age, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, appearance, wealth, occupation, hobbies, or other identity.
- Inciting conflict, manufacturing opposition, or organizing users to attack others.

## 10. Rights and Originality

Flag:

- Unauthorized use of video, image, music, voice, text, subtitles, fan art, game footage, anime clips, film/TV clips, or creator material.
- Low-effort搬运/二创 that adds no meaningful commentary, analysis, or creative transformation.
- Portrait/name/privacy/reputation/trademark/copyright infringement.
- Using platform, brand, or creator identifiers to imply official relation.
