## Severity Hints

- `Blocker`: illegal/prohibited content, porn, gambling, violent terror, minor harm, phishing/malware, suicide/self-harm instruction, prohibited industries, clear infringement/privacy leak, off-platform contact/QR/direct transaction risk, cheating/fake engagement, unqualified medical/finance/game content, severe false marketing.
- `High`: sexual lowbrow, blood/horror, hostile identity attack, manufactured opposition, fake persona/story, unverified efficacy/experience, commercial diversion, copied content, private-message funnel, fake data or engagement manipulation.
- `Medium`: context-dependent marketing, weak disclosure, missing proof, unverifiable source, topic likely to trigger community trust issues.
- `Low`: mild title/cover/wording/quality issue.

## Xiaohongshu-Specific Orientation

The platform emphasizes:

- 真诚分享: authentic experience, not fake persona/story or fabricated use effect.
- 友好互动: no insults, discrimination, cyberbullying, harassment, or manufactured opposition.
- 有序经营: commercial content should be compliant, disclosed where needed, not deceptive, not malicious competition, and not off-platform risky diversion.

When evidence is weak, Xiaohongshu reviews should be stricter about "真实性" and "种草可信度" than generic short-video reviews.

## 1. Illegal and Prohibited Content

Flag:

- National/social-security illegal information, anti-constitutional content, national-security or sovereignty risk, state secrets, damage to national honor/interests.
- Ethnic/regional hatred or discrimination; religious-policy violations, cults, superstition.
- Harm to public morals or traditional culture; insulting/misusing heroic martyrs; beautifying invaders, invasion wars, criminals, or crime.
- Rumors or false information disturbing social order.
- Illegal assembly or public-order disruption.
- Obscene language damaging public order and morals.

## 2. Pornographic, Sexual, and Lowbrow Content

Flag:

- Direct exposure or description of sexual body parts.
- Nudity or covering private parts only with limbs/objects.
- Explicit or implicit sexual behavior, teasing, humiliating sexual framing.
- Vulgar or teasing titles that bait clicks.
- Sexual language describing sexual acts, process, or methods.
- Pornographic/lowbrow links, pictures, text, video/audio, deleted film clips, erotic anime.
- One-night stands, partner swapping, sexual abuse, sex services, illegal sexual-drug ads, STD treatment ads.

For lifestyle/fashion/fitness/beauty content, review camera focus, cover, title, body-part emphasis, pose, and comments/CTA together.

## 3. Violence, Terror, and Discomfort

Flag:

- Promoting violence, murder, terrorism, extremism, or criminal instruction.
- Real scenes of people or animals killed, disabled, shot, stabbed, tortured, abused, or injured.
- Bloody conflict causing physical discomfort.
- Frightening, unsettling, or highly disturbing scenes.

## 4. Gambling and Illegal Services

Flag:

- Lottery/gambling/betting information, gambling-platform links, QR codes, invitation codes.
- Gambling devices or cheating tools: slot machines, gambling machines, remote dice, see-through poker, etc.
- Game-coin recovery, intermediaries, or other online-gambling services.
- Gambling skills, methods, tutorials, or any betting activity.

## 5. Minors, Self-Harm, and Safety

Flag:

- Minor marriage/pregnancy.
- Inducing minors into illegal/criminal behavior or damaging minors' rights/health.
- Revealing minors' privacy or damaging minors' dignity.
- Suicide content, self-harm, dangerous action, or imitation-inducing behavior.
- Phishing, malware, third-party forged redirect links, virus sites, or unsafe links.

## 6. Prohibited and Restricted Industries

Flag brand/account/business content involving:

- Unqualified medical/health, healthcare products, contact lenses, beauty devices.
- Financial insurance or games without required industry qualification.
- "三无" micro-businesses, cosmetic surgery, private detective, paternity testing, bitcoin/crypto trading, IVF, fetal sex identification, surrogacy, tobacco and peripheral products, and other prohibited industries.

If account/brand qualification is not visible, mark `待核验` and treat product marketing as at least `High` for medical/finance/game categories.

## 7. Infringement and Unfriendly Behavior

Flag:

- Mocking, provoking, humiliating, insulting, discriminating, threatening, blackmailing, hate speech, malicious flame war.
- Encouraging users to attack, isolate, or target another user.
- Identity-based discrimination or attack based on ethnicity, race, religion, sexual orientation, gender, age, region, physical traits, etc.
- Repetitive similar notes/comments/private messages for harassment or marketing.
- Non-original content copied or collected from the internet without authorization; copyright, portrait, IP, or trademark risk.
- Privacy leaks, voyeurism, upskirt, accidental exposure,偷拍, or intimate imagery.
- Fake rumors, unsupported facts, impersonation, fake affiliation or fake experience.

## 8. Commercial Diversion and Improper Information

Flag:

- Non-regular/private sales intent: "可代购", "找我购买", "转卖", "拼单" and similar.
- Noncompliant contact or purchase methods: phone number, mobile number, Xianyu, QQ, Taobao shop, Taobao password, WeChat link, micro-shop, QR code, purchase-method watermark.
- Deceptive malicious marketing: fake activity, impersonation, fake offer, fake scarcity, fake benefit.
- Coordinated low-quality marketing with similar content across accounts.
- Pseudoscience or content against scientific common sense.
- Content against public order/morals or socialist core values.

## 9. Cheating, Inducement, and Harassment

Flag:

- Buying likes, saves, comments, shares, follows, search words, note ghostwriting, ranking services, or SEO-like services.
- Switching accounts/devices, fake devices, automation, or software control to create fake engagement.
- Mobilizing related parties to boost engagement or post similar notes.
- Mutual-like/follow groups.
- Inducing or deceiving users to like, save, share, or follow, such as "双击有惊喜" or "关注领红包".
- Editing a high-performing note to swap in a different product/brand for malicious promotion.
- Mass posting WeChat/QQ/Taobao contacts, repetitive marketing content, or private-message spam.
- Framing competitors, evading punishment through new/renamed accounts.

Potential handling includes removing fake engagement, no recommendation, clearing note data, public labels, warning, temporary ban on posting, account-wide no recommendation, public labels on account page, or legal pursuit.

## 10. Commercial Collaboration and Advertising

Flag:

- Commercial notes arising from payment, gifting, trial invitations, brand contracts, special writing, tasting/check-in, posting for rebate, lottery, or other benefit.
- Brand-account commercial notes not using required brand-topic or reporting process.
- Commercial notes/comments/private messages containing illegal content, harassment, attack, or ad diversion.
- False experience, fake before/after effect, undisclosed promotion, malicious competitor comparison, fake authority, fake data, or unverifiable recommendation.

Request proof: cooperation brief, disclosure method, platform reporting record, brand topic/reporting flow, product qualification, price/gift/activity consistency.

## 11. Community Covenant 2.0 Checks

Flag content that undermines:

- 真诚分享: fake persona, fake story, fake experience, fake review, fabricated comparison, undisclosed paid promotion.
- 友好互动: cyberbullying, insults, harassment, identity attack, group opposition.
- 有序经营: false marketing, malicious competition, off-platform transaction diversion, private traffic traps, commercial spam.

Pay special attention to manufactured opposition: gender opposition, regional discrimination, occupation bias, poverty/wealth conflict, hobby/consumer-class contempt, "stand in line and fight" framing, anxiety-selling through group labels.

## 12. Case-Informed Xiaohongshu Checks

Use `references/cases/xiaohongshu.md` when reviewing:

- Suspected 伪素人, planted review, or comment-matrix marketing.
- Notes involving minors, especially dating inducement, revealing clothing, self-harm, violence, privacy, or commercial exploitation.
- Account limiting, note non-indexing, 小眼睛为0, appeal, or "违规后怎么办" questions.
- Cross-platform creator discussions found through Xiaohongshu search, such as 微信公众号封号, 视频号限流, 抖音举报/封号服务, 快手被盗申诉, or B站小黑屋; treat them as discussion samples, not official rules.
- Repeated comment scripts such as 互看, 停留几秒, 点赞回复, copied appeal wording, or coordinated "求链接/已买/有效" comments.

For 限流/不收录, do not infer a violation from low traffic alone. Mark `待核验` and ask for platform notice screenshots, note-status screenshots, edit history, promotion/薯条 status, commercial disclosure, source authorization, and product/qualification proof.
