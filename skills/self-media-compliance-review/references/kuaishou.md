## Severity Hints

- `Blocker`: illegal/prohibited information, pornography, gambling, fraud, terrorism/extremism, serious violence/gore, illegal goods, privacy leak, account theft/sale/rent, paid ban/unban services, severe infringement, harmful minors content, off-platform transaction funnel for high-risk goods/services.
- `High`: sexual lowbrow, insults/harassment, repeated spam or mutual-follow/like requests, external-platform diversion, unauthorized copyright in live/video, fake official identity, fake benefits, dangerous behavior, e-commerce contact exposure, live-cover sexualization or misery marketing.
- `Medium`: unclear source/authorization, weak commercial disclosure, vague appeal evidence, user-profile wording that may look like diversion, comment risk that depends on context.
- `Low`: minor title/cover/comment wording or UX issue.

## 1. Community Content

Flag:

- Illegal, political, violent, terrorist, extremist, cult, superstition, gambling, fraud, illegal finance, or illegal-goods content.
- Pornographic, sexual, vulgar, or lowbrow content, including sexual transactions, sexualized text/audio/visuals, or adult-resource promotion.
- Rights violations: insults, defamation, discrimination, privacy leaks, human flesh search, unauthorized reposting/carrying, plagiarism, or impersonation.
- Harmful minors content, dangerous behavior, self-harm, violence, bullying, or content likely to induce imitation.
- Rumors, misinformation, fake public-event information, misleading edits, or clickbait that disturbs order.
- Repeated low-quality marketing, suspicious services, or content whose main purpose is traffic diversion rather than user value.

## 2. User Profile and Account Presentation

Flag user profile material containing:

- Illegal, superstition, pyramid-scheme, gambling, firearms, porn/lowbrow, or illegal-resource information.
- QR codes, watermarks, WeChat/phone/contact variants, third-party platform accounts, or other off-platform diversion.
- Minor-harm information, privacy exposure, harassment, blood/violence/self-harm, lowbrow material, or official/person/celebrity impersonation.
- Inducement through cash, goods, virtual rewards, tasks, or off-platform joining.
- Illegal ads, fake/low-quality goods, game boosting/item trading, no-copyright film/video resources, or illegal part-time/recruitment information.
- For e-commerce/live-commerce accounts: contact methods such as mobile number, WeChat, QR code, `V我`, `主页有微`, `主页有惊喜`, or third-party accounts.

## 3. Comments and Interaction

Flag comments that contain:

- Illegal or politically improper information, cult/superstition, illegal-goods sale, insults, discrimination, provocation, harassment, or spam.
- Mutual-follow/mutual-like requests, repeated ads, symbols flooding, or meaningless long comments.
- External-platform links, third-party account redirection, lotteries, red packets, benefits, or group-joining inducement.
- Sexual/lowbrow comments, escort/prostitution information, sex jokes, or sexual implication.
- High-risk ads, including weight loss, medical beauty, online part-time work, finance/wealth management, cheating services, and overseas matchmaking.
- Harmful minors content, rumors, curses, or engagement-inducing superstition.

## 4. Live Streams, Live Covers, and Commerce

Flag:

- Promotion, recommendation, or diversion to other platforms.
- Unauthorized livestreaming or rebroadcasting of copyrighted works.
- Off-platform transactions, including showing WeChat/phone/contact information or directing users to profile contact methods instead of platform transaction tools.
- Live-commerce content with no real person, no meaningful interaction, sleeping, black screen, looped media, or behavior unrelated to the product/service.
- Unofficial lottery or reward mechanics, including screenshot lottery, fan-list/order-list draw, fan-group lottery, or off-platform lottery.
- Impersonating officials, using official-like language without authorization, or distorting platform rules.
- Live covers with misery-persona bait, dating/sexual implication, tight/transparent/revealing clothing, minors in inappropriate dress, non-mainstream relationship sensationalism, or discomfort content.

## 5. Ban Appeal and Reporting

Official appeal/help signal:

- For a banned account, the official help page says to use 快手 App `菜单 -> 头像 -> 解封申诉` and submit materials according to the page prompts. For questions about ban reason or appeal result, contact customer service through the help page.
- The official report-handling page says reports can come through app reports, phone, visit, email, or regulator transfer; reports should be accepted and checked, and confirmed violations may be handled by limiting exposure, deletion, or bans.

When a user asks `快手封号/限流/直播违规/禁言怎么办`, request:

- Official notice screenshot, account id, video/live/comment id, handling time, current restricted function, and appeal result.
- Login/security proof if account theft is claimed: device/IP/location alerts, password reset, operator changes, third-party tool access, and timeline.
- Source authorization, original files, product/service qualification, e-commerce link consistency, profile/contact screenshots, live replay/clips, and comment/private-message evidence.

Appeal guidance:

- Do not promise unblocking or quote a success rate.
- Do not recommend paid unban services, copied appeal scripts, account switching, mass reporting, or workaround contact variants.
- Fix the risky element first, then appeal with specific facts and proof.
